About CrossPlay Central

CrossPlay Central was created in 2023 to bring gamers from all platforms together. Whether you play on PlayStation, Xbox, PC, Mobile or Nintendo Switch - this is your hub for connecting across platforms, sharing experiences, and discovering new cross-platform gaming opportunities.

Our Story

The idea sparked when our founder, Alex Rivera, struggled to find teammates for crossplay games. Despite playing the same games, platform barriers kept friends apart. What began as a Discord server in 2022 has grown into the web's premier cross-platform gaming community with over 250,000 registered members.

What We Do

  • Maintain the most comprehensive crossplay game database
  • Host platform-agnostic tournaments and events
  • Provide technical support for crossplay connectivity
  • Develop tools to enhance cross-platform gaming
  • Advocate for more crossplay options in games

Our Mission

We believe gaming should unite people, not divide them by platform. Our mission is to break down barriers between gaming communities through:

Accessibility

Community

Building the most welcoming cross-platform gaming community

Innovation

Developing new ways to enhance cross-platform play
